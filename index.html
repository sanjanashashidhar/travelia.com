Travel Tips for a Smooth Journey

  • Plan your itinerary in advance to make the most of your trip.
  • Keep both physical and digital copies of essential documents.
  • Check weather forecasts and pack accordingly.
  • Inform your bank about international travel to avoid card issues.
  • Stay hydrated and take breaks during long journeys.
  • Use secure payment methods for bookings and purchases.

Traveller's Checklist

  • Valid Passport and Visa
  • Travel Insurance Documents
  • Flight Tickets and Hotel Bookings
  • Emergency Contact Numbers
  • Medicines and First Aid Kit
  • Phone Charger and Power Bank
  • Local Currency and Credit Cards
  • Travel Guidebook or App
